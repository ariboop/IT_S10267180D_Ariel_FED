About them

They are a 4th gen K-Pop boy band that was formed during a 2017 reality show and debuted in the entertainment company called JYPE. They have eight members, Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Han Jisung, HyunJin, Felix, Seungmin and I.N. They also had nine members before one of them, Woojin, left due to personal reasons in 2019, only 2 years after their debut. They mainly produce their own music with the three songwriters, Bang Chan, Changbin and Han Jisung going by 3racha while the others will also often help with songwriting. 3racha has also won an award of "Best Creator" during the 2023 Asia Artist Awards.

Their Logo

While their group name "Stray Kids" was given with no meaning behind it, the members conceptualised it themselves. The name originally meant a lost child who wants to chase their dreams and evolved to represent the idea of finding a way out of the ordinary together.

Here, the "SKZ" is meant to represent a keyhole, symbolsing the group's mission to unlock new possibilties and break down barriers in the music industry. The Z stands for Zone, making SKZ literally mean Stray Kids Zone. It refers to a place or zone that metaphorically means a place we're people like us, Stays, can lose ourselves and be who we are.

The lightstick

The V1 of the light stick was released for Stray Kids's first concert, as part of the world tour District 9 : Unlock in 2019. It is composed of a white handle with a crooked edge symbolizing "unstable way", holding up a spinnable red and silver "compass without direction" symbolizing "Stray Kids's way". It is a compass because a lot of Skz's music is about the struggles young people might have, and the broken compass might have symbolized that not everything is going to go the way you want it to go. Because of the broken compass, Skz had to go through many difficulties to get to their destination.
