Personal Life

Lee Min-ho was born on October 25, 1998 in Gimpo, South Korea. He grew up in Daegot-myeon.

He adopted his first cat Soonie in 2011 and his second cat Doongie around 2013.

When he was in middle school, he watched a dance video and found it cool. He tried to dance in front of the mirror and thought he was good at it, so he asked his mother if he could take dance classes, and she approved. He joined Souldance Studio in Seoul and stayed there for five years, attending classes like Bico Lim's.

While dancing and completing his education, he had several part time jobs in order to pay transport fares and cram schools, such as handing out flyers, teaching at a dance academy, and working in a barbeque restaurant.

He graduated from Gimpo Jeil Technical High School in computer sciences. He passed an entrance exam at the Chugye University For The Arts with a self choreography and joined the campus on March 2017. He kept attending even after debuting, but left at some point.

He adopted his third cat, Dori, in February 2019.

He is currently enrolled at Gukje Cyber University under the Department of Entertainment Studies.

In November 2023, he participated in the "Love Your W" campaign by W Korea to raise awareness about breast cancer.

On February 29, 2024, it was announced that Lee Know had donated 100 million won to World Vision's "Global Hunger Crisis Response" project to help children face food shortages in the poorest countries, and was appointed as a member of the "Bob Pierce" donors club.


Dance

Lee Know's main dance genre (and his favorite) is hip-hop. Earlier, he had a recognizable style of the Souldance Studio he studied at. His dancing is characterized by a neat isolation of body parts and the way his movements follow the beat neatly. His style is best showcased on songs that require precise yet hip movements such as "Easy" and "Super Bowl". Thanks to his background in martial arts, he is sometimes in charge of performing stunts, especially during Kingdom: Legendary War stages and in the song "S-Class".

Lee Know is also called the group's "dance leader" by Bang Chan since debut. Due to his professional background in dancing, he helps the other members with getting the choreography right during practice. He is also involved in choreography, and choreographed his solo dance video "DAWN" and the group's 2018 MAMA FANS' CHOICE in JAPAN performance.


Vocals and Rap

Lee Know is mainly considered as a vocalist. He is especially known for his falsettos, showcased in "Levanter" and "TASTE". However, he was always midway between a rapper and vocalist. Even though he never had an official rap role, J.Y. Park asked him to take rap parts during the survival show as he deemed his voice too weak for singing due to the lack of a training period.

In the line distribution, he thus has flexibles lines, sometimes rapping (verse, chorus) and sometimes singing (pre-chorus, bridge). Some rap parts he did became some of his most famous lines, like his introductory rap in "Boxer".

Visuals

Lee Know is part of the visual line along Hyunjin and Felix. He is nicknamed "Unreachable Visual" since debut thanks to his sharp features like his eyes and nose, but also an overall softness to his looks which makes him fit into the "flower boy" archetype. Animals he is associated with are the cat and the bunny.


Predebut

Lee Min-ho pursued dancing professionally. He part of two dance teams within Souldance: Cupcakes and Browniebros. In 2016, he performed at World of Dance as a front row guest with Cupcakes. Around the same time, he became a backup dancer for BTS, from the songs "Fire" to "Spring Day" and participated in the "Wings Tour" in early 2017. During his backup dancing career, he realized that he wanted to stand at the center of the stage, so he became a singer.

Minho had auditioned for JYP Entertainment back in 2015 after being noticed during a global dance event, but he didnt hear from them and thought he had failed. He continued his life as a dancer, until he was finally called back two years later. He joined JYPE on July 15, 2017, making the last member of Stray Kids to join the lineup.

His individual picture and name were officially revealed for his participation in the upcoming survival show Stray Kids. He was introduced with the role "Dance". During the show, he was eliminated on episode 4 because he lacked confidence and made mistakes while rapping. Through the audience voting in favor of the 9-member group over the 7-member group, it was then confirmed that Min-ho would debut with the boy group Stray Kids.
