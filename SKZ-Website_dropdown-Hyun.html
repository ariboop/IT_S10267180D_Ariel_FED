Personal Life

He grew up with a pet dog, Kkomi. As a kid, he visited Las Vegas, where he used his English name, Sam.

On November 15, 2018, he took the suneung exam.

He graduated from the School of Performing Arts (SOPA) on February 15, 2019.

He is currently enrolled at the Global Cyber University, studying Practical English.

On March 30, 2023, Hyunjin was appointed to "The Promise" donor club as a major donor for having donated over 100 million won to help the victims of the earthquakes in Turkey and Syria.

In November 2023, he participated in the 18th "Love Your W" campaign by W Korea to raise awareness about breast cancer.

On April 4, 2024, Hyunjin donated 100 million won to support the hearing impaired through the organization The Snail of Love, becoming a member of the "Soul Leader" donor club.

Dance

Hyunjin's dancing is known for its theatricality and control over his body movements and facial expressions. He showcases two distinct dancing styles, switching swiftly and seamlessly from a fluid and relaxed style to a jerky and sharp style. He always gives his own flair to the group's choreographies and easily draws attention. As such, he has become a key element for the performance aspect of the group.

Hyunjin is also involved in choreography, especially for his solo dance covers such as "Play With Fire". He also gives his input when it comes to the group's live performances. He participated in arranging the stage for the group version of "Red Lights" and suggested to perform "GET LIT" at 2023 Asian Artist Awards to showcase something new.


Rapping and Songwriting

In the line distribution, Hyunjin is mainly in charge of rap-based parts. Even though his voice is usually high, he has a signature lazy, low voice rapping which is showcased on songs like "VENOM", "Muddy Water" and "Chk Chk Boom". He often gets verses and choruses. Since 2021, Hyunjin got into songwriting and works a lot with Bang Chan to make demos, and since 2022, he started releasing solo SKZ-RECORD songs to showcase his own artistry. He likes writing songs about love and crushes.


Visuals

Hyunjin is part of the visual line along Lee Know and Felix. Body wise, he has long legs and good proportions. Face-wise, he is characterized by long horizontal eyes, thick lips, and a sharp jawline, often called "ice prince" or "chic" type of visuals.

Since 2020 he has been showing off trendy looks carried by a signature long hair, notably blonde or black. A large amount of people, including himself, prefering long over short hair. Since then, he rarely ever cuts his hair short.

When he smiles, he looks much rounder and softer, which has given his nickname "Dumpling". Animals he is associated to are the llama, the weasel and the ferret.

Predebut

While he was on a shopping trip with his mother in middle school, Hyunjin was scouted by JYP Entertainment to become a trainee. He then joined JYPE without any skill and trained for two years. As a rookie, he got interested in dancing by watching the senior trainees performing on stage, and practiced more by studying various types of dance at the School of Performing Arts (SOPA).

His individual picture and name were officially revealed for his participation in the upcoming survival show Stray Kids. He was introduced with the roles "Rap" and "Dance". On the final episode of the show on December 19, 2017, it was confirmed that Hyunjin would debut with the boy group Stray Kids.


Skzoo

Jiniret is Hyunjin's skzoo. Jiniret's design was inspired by Hyunjin's resemblance with a ferret in his facial features, height, and for his fluid dancing.

His name is a combination of "Jinnie" (Hyunjin's nickname) and "ferret".
