Personal Life

He was introduced to baseball by his grand-father. He initially dreamed of becoming a baseball player but he had to stop because of an injury and other problems. Still, he liked singing ever since he was little. In the fourth grade of elementary school, he went to a nursery rhyme contest, won a prize, and joined a choir. In middle school, when he was thinking about what he should do to be happy in life, he thought about "music", so he studied and practiced singing to appeal to his parents.

He learned English when he went to Los Angeles for 3 months.

He graduated from Cheongdam High School on February 14 , 2019.

Around 2020, Seungmin scouted for his own vocal teacher outside of JYP Entertainment to improve his singing skills and started attending the classes of Voice Tuning.

On May 8, 2023, he was invited to throw the first pitch for the Lotte Giants in Busan, but the match was cancelled because of bad weather.

On March 18, 2024, he threw the first pitch for the exhibition match between Team Korea and Los Angeles Dodgers at the Gocheok Sky Dome.


Predebut

While in high school, Seungmin participated in JYPE's 13th Open Audition, encouraged by his school friend and JYP trainee Lee Daehwi, and placed 2nd. He joined JYP Entertainment in March 2017, which makes him the penultimate member to join the lineup.

His individual picture and name were officially revealed by the company on October 13, 2017 at noon, for his participation in the upcoming survival show Stray Kids. He was introduced with the role "Vocal". On the final episode of the show on December 19, 2017, it was confirmed that Seungmin would debut in the boy group Stray Kids.


Fashion

In November 2023, Seungmin appeared on the special cover of Nylon Japan, in collaboration with Coach for their holiday campaign in Japan.

On March 1, 2024, Seungmin made his fashion week debut attending the Loewe Fall Winter 2024 Women's Show held in Paris. British Vogue featured him in their article on best front-row styles of PFW. In May 2024, he made his Met Gala debut with Stray Kids as the guest of Tommy Hilfiger.
